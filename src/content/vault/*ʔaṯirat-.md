## Myths and Legends

**Divine Queen**
As consort of [[*Ê”il-]], *Ê”aá¹¯irat- held a position of supreme authority in the Proto-Semitic divine hierarchy. Her role as mother of the gods established her as a central figure in creation mythology.

**Mother of Gods**
In her capacity as divine mother, she was considered the progenitor of numerous deities, establishing divine dynasties that would later be reflected in various Semitic pantheons.
